Enjoy mind-bending theoretical challenges? Or perhaps you're interested in note taking,
organization and how it shapes our lives? You're in the right place.

I'm Mariusz, lead developer of [Neorg](https://github.com/nvim-neorg/neorg), a modern
attempt at creating a truly comprehensive organizational suite for Neovim and [Lux](https://github.com/lumen-oss/lux),
a next-generation embeddable package manager for Lua.

In this blog I discuss:
- Technical challenges and releases of my projects
- The foundations of what make note taking tools effective
- Interesting bits and bobs about human nature and how we perceive new information
- Tonnes of technical challenges I encountered when writing code for two major ecosystems!

Take a wander around the digital garden, and I hope you enjoy your stay :)
